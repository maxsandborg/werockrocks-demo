The Complete Guide to Kava: What It Is, How It Works & Why Tampa Bay Is Obsessed

By Exotic Smokes • March 2026 • 12 min read

Walk into any Exotic Smokes location after 8 PM and you'll notice something: half the seats aren't taken by people smoking — they're taken by people sipping. Kava, the ancient South Pacific root drink, has quietly become one of the biggest draws at our bars. But if you've never tried it, you probably have questions. This guide covers everything.

What Exactly Is Kava?

Kava (Piper methysticum) is a plant native to the South Pacific islands — Fiji, Vanuatu, Tonga, and Hawaii. For over 3,000 years, Pacific Islander communities have prepared kava as a ceremonial and social drink. The active compounds, called kavalactones, interact with the brain's GABA receptors to produce a calming, mildly euphoric effect — without impairing cognitive function the way alcohol does.

Think of it as the opposite of coffee: where caffeine amps you up, kava brings you down to a grounded, social, relaxed state. You're clear-headed but loose. Talkative but calm. It's no wonder kava bars are becoming the preferred "third place" for people who want to be social without drinking alcohol.

How Does Kava Work?

The 18 kavalactones identified in kava root work synergistically to produce effects that typically onset within 15-20 minutes. The primary mechanisms include GABA-A receptor modulation (producing anxiolytic effects), dopamine pathway interaction (mild mood elevation), and sodium channel modulation (muscle relaxation). Unlike alcohol, kava does not impair motor function or cognitive clarity at normal doses — which is why many people drive to kava bars without concern.

Types of Kava You'll Find at Exotic Smokes

  • Traditional Prep (Shell): Ground kava root strained through water. Earthy, slightly bitter, authentic. This is how Pacific Islanders have consumed it for millennia. We serve it in coconut shells to honor the tradition.
  • Kava Tea: A lighter preparation that's easier on the palate for first-timers. Blended with natural flavors to smooth out the earthiness while keeping the full kavalactone profile.
  • Kava Smoothies & Blends: Our most popular option for newcomers — kava blended with tropical fruits, chocolate, or peanut butter. The flavors mask the root taste completely while delivering the same relaxation.
  • Kratom Options: We also carry kratom drinks — a different plant entirely (Mitragyna speciosa from Southeast Asia) that offers energizing effects at low doses and relaxation at higher doses. Our bartenders can help you navigate the differences.

What to Expect on Your First Visit

First-timers at our kava bar usually notice a slight numbing of the lips and tongue within 5-10 minutes — this is completely normal and comes from the kavalactones. Within 20 minutes, most people describe a wave of calm settling in. Conversations flow easier. Tension in the shoulders melts away. The ambient music sounds better. You'll understand why our regulars come back 3-4 times a week.

We recommend starting with a single shell of traditional kava or one of our flavored blends. Our bartenders are trained to guide you through the menu based on what you're looking for — whether that's deep relaxation, social energy, or something in between.

Why Tampa Bay Is a Kava Hotspot

Florida has more kava bars per capita than any other state in the US. The Tampa Bay area alone has seen a 300%+ increase in kava establishments over the past five years. The reasons are straightforward: Florida's climate attracts a health-conscious, outdoor-focused population that's increasingly looking for alcohol alternatives. The state's sober-curious movement is massive, and kava fits perfectly — it's social, it's relaxing, it's legal, and there's no hangover.

At Exotic Smokes, we've leaned into this by making our kava bar a centerpiece of the experience rather than an afterthought. Our Wesley Chapel, Tampa, and Clearwater locations all feature dedicated bar areas with trained kava bartenders, comfortable lounge seating, and a curated playlist that sets the right vibe whether you're visiting at 2 PM or 2 AM.

Hookah 101: A Beginner's Guide to Shisha, Flavors & Lounge Etiquette

By Exotic Smokes • March 2026 • 10 min read

Hookah has been a social tradition for over 500 years, originating in India and spreading throughout the Middle East, North Africa, and now the entire world. At Exotic Smokes, our hookah lounge is one of the most popular spaces in the building — and for good reason. There's something irreplaceable about sharing a hookah with friends in a comfortable, low-lit setting with good music. If you've never tried it, here's everything you need to know.

How Does a Hookah Work?

A hookah (also called shisha, narghile, or waterpipe) consists of several components that work together: the bowl sits on top and holds flavored tobacco (shisha), which is heated by charcoal. Smoke travels down the stem, through water in the base (which cools and filters it), and up through the hose to the mouthpiece. The water filtration is what makes hookah smoke notably smoother and cooler than other smoking methods.

At Exotic Smokes, we use premium handcrafted hookahs with high-quality components. Our staff assembles each hookah to order, packing the bowl with the perfect density and managing the heat so you get thick, flavorful clouds from the first pull to the last.

Shisha Flavors: What We Carry

Shisha (the flavored tobacco) is where the magic happens. Modern shisha comes in hundreds of flavor profiles, from fruity to minty to creamy to exotic. At Exotic Smokes, we stock the most respected brands in the industry:

  • Al Fakher: The world's best-selling shisha brand. Known for consistent quality and classic flavors. Our top sellers are Watermelon Mint, Double Apple, and Grape Mint. Al Fakher is the safe bet for both beginners and experienced smokers.
  • Starbuzz: A premium American brand known for bold, unique flavor combinations. Blue Mist (blueberry mint) and Pirates Cave are customer favorites. Starbuzz tends to produce denser clouds.
  • Fumari: The "craft beer" of shisha — known for exceptionally juicy, sweet flavors. White Gummy Bear and Ambrosia are legendary in the hookah community.
  • Tangiers: The advanced smoker's choice. Tangiers uses a unique packing method and delivers incredibly bold, long-lasting flavor sessions. Not for beginners, but beloved by hookah enthusiasts.

Best Flavor Combinations

One of the best parts of hookah culture is mixing flavors to create custom blends. Here are some combinations our staff recommends for beginners:

  • Watermelon Mint + Grape: A refreshing, fruity blend that's universally loved. The mint adds coolness without overpowering the fruit.
  • Double Apple + Mint: The classic Middle Eastern combination. Anise-forward with a clean menthol finish. This is the most traditional hookah flavor in the world.
  • Blueberry + Vanilla: A creamy, dessert-like experience. Sweet and smooth, great for evening sessions.
  • Peach + Lemon: Bright and tangy. Perfect for daytime sessions when you want something light and energizing.

Hookah Lounge Etiquette

Whether you're visiting Exotic Smokes or any hookah lounge, there are a few unwritten rules that show respect for the culture and make the experience better for everyone:

  • Don't light cigarettes from the hookah coals. This is considered disrespectful in hookah culture and can also be a safety issue.
  • Set the hose down, don't pass it directly. When you're done with your turn, place the hose on the table rather than handing it mouthpiece-first to someone else.
  • Don't blow smoke in people's faces. Basic respect applies in lounge settings too. Exhale upward or to the side.
  • Tip your hookah attendant. At Exotic Smokes, our staff assembles, manages, and maintains your hookah throughout the session. If you enjoyed the experience, a tip is appreciated.
  • Relax and take your time. A hookah session typically lasts 45-90 minutes. This isn't a rush-through experience — it's meant to be savored with conversation and good company.

Why Exotic Smokes' Hookah Lounge Is Different

Most smoke shops that offer hookah treat it as a side feature — a few hookahs tucked into a corner. At Exotic Smokes, we've built dedicated lounge spaces designed specifically for the hookah experience. Comfortable seating, ambient lighting, curated playlists, and attentive staff who manage your coals and flavor throughout the session. Whether you're coming with a group of friends on a Friday night or stopping by solo for a quiet session after work at 3 AM, the experience is consistent and premium.

All three of our locations — Wesley Chapel, Tampa, and Clearwater — feature full hookah lounges open 24/7. Walk-ins are always welcome, no reservation needed.
