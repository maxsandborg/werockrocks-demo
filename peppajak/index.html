Our Story

Made Bold, Served Fresh

PeppaJak brings bold, crave-worthy American comfort food to The KRATE at the Grove in Wesley Chapel. Every burger is hand-patted with 100% Angus beef and cooked to order — never frozen, never rushed.

From our signature wagyu blend burgers to our legendary hot honey wings, we're all about big flavors and fresh ingredients. Our house-made sauces are what set us apart — each one crafted to bring the perfect balance of heat, sweet, and savory.
