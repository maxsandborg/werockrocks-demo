About Meridian

40 Years of Excellence

Founded in 1984, Meridian Yacht Group has been at the forefront of luxury yacht brokerage for four decades. Our founders recognized an opportunity to revolutionize the yacht market through integrity, expertise, and client-centric service.

Today, we represent over 2,000 successful vessel transactions and maintain a roster of the world's most prestigious yachts. Our global network spans three continents, enabling us to serve clients and broker vessels with unmatched efficiency.

We believe that luxury yacht ownership should be exceptional at every stage—from initial consultation through final handover and beyond.

Our Story

1984
Meridian founded in Fort Lauderdale by maritime visionaries committed to yacht brokerage excellence.
1995
Opened Monaco office to serve European and Mediterranean clientele.
2005

2012
Expanded to Singapore, establishing presence in Asian luxury markets.
