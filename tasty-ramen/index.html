Our Story

Authentically Japanese.
In Every Bowl.

At Tasty Ramen, we believe great ramen starts with the broth. Our tonkotsu is simmered low and slow for 18 hours, extracting every ounce of rich, collagen-packed flavor from premium pork bones.

Located in the heart of Wesley Chapel at KRATE at the Grove, we serve ramen the way it's meant to be — bold, comforting, and crafted with care. From our signature tonkotsu to our fiery spicy miso, every bowl is a journey to Japan.
