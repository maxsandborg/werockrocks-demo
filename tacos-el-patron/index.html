AUTHENTICITY
YOU CAN TASTE

What started as a single food truck in 2019 has grown into three locations across Florida — Wesley Chapel, Dade City, and the Tampa Premium Outlets in Lutz.

At Tacos El Patrón, every tortilla is made by hand, every salsa is made from scratch, and every meal is prepared with the same passion and pride that's been passed down through generations of Mexican cooking.

From our signature Birria Tacos to our slow-cooked Carnitas and freshly grilled Asada — this is the real deal. No shortcuts. No compromises.
