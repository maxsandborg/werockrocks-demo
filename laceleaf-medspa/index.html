Common Questions

Everything You Want to Know

We believe an informed client is a confident client. If your question isn't answered here, ask us during your consultation — no question is too small.

Ask Us Directly
Botox (botulinum toxin type A) has been FDA-approved for cosmetic use since 2002 and has an extensive safety record with millions of treatments performed annually. When administered by a trained, licensed provider, serious complications are rare. Common temporary side effects include mild bruising, redness, or swelling at injection sites. We conduct thorough consultations to assess your candidacy and ensure treatment is right for you.
Results vary based on the filler type, treatment area, your metabolism, and lifestyle. Generally, hyaluronic acid fillers last 9–18 months in areas like the lips, and 12–24 months in the cheeks and nasolabial folds. Denser products used for structural support often last longer. Your provider will give you a realistic timeline during your consultation and recommend a maintenance schedule.
Most of our treatments have minimal to no downtime. Botox and fillers may cause mild bruising and swelling for 24–72 hours but most clients return to normal activities immediately. RF microneedling may cause 1–3 days of redness and mild peeling. Laser treatments vary — IPL may cause temporary darkening of treated spots before they shed. We'll always walk you through post-care so you know exactly what to expect.
Natural results are our specialty and our highest priority. We use a conservative, artful approach — enhancing your best features without changing the essence of who you are. Our injectors are trained to work with your anatomy and facial proportions. Our goal is for people to notice how refreshed and confident you look, not that you "had something done."
That's exactly what your complimentary consultation is for. We conduct a thorough skin assessment, listen to your goals, and recommend the treatments most likely to deliver the results you want — at a pace and budget that works for you. We never pressure clients or recommend treatments that aren't genuinely beneficial for your skin.
Yes — we partner with CareCredit® and Cherry™ to offer flexible monthly payment options. Most applications are completed in minutes with instant decisions.
