Our Story

Created by indigenous women, for everyone

Born inside a shipping container at KRATE at the Grove, The Rebellious Hippie isn't your typical wellness shop. We're a holistic lifestyle destination with an urban edge — where ancestral knowledge of plant medicine meets the rhythm of modern life.

We source ethically, craft intentionally, and share freely. Every herb we sell, every soap we pour, every plant we nurture carries the spirit of generations who healed with what the earth provided.
